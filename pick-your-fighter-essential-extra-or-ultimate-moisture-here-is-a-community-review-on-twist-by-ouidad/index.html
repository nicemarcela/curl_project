Pick Your Fighter: Essential, Extra or Ultimate Moisture? Here is a Community Review on Twist by Ouidad.

#gifted@twisthair

TWIST by Ouidad is a new brand that is all about moisture. You might be wondering if Twist and Ouidad are the same brands; No, they are two different brands but are part of the same family. TWIST has a pure moisture approach, as they claim "They’re redefining the curl conversation by putting moisture first because moisture-balanced hair is what your curls need to thrive". Their product lines have a moisture scale that you can mix and match to find your perfect blend, allowing you to customize a routine that works for your unique and personal curl journey. You can find yours through this Hair Quiz.

I got the chance to try the products1 from their three scales: Essential Moisture, Extra Moisture, and Ultimate Moisture. To make this post as straightforward as possible I will just review my favorites! In this post, you will also find the community's opinion2 (of course 🥰), and the overall rating of the brand. Let's get reviewing... #GetMoist

ESSENTIAL MOISTURE - Weightless moisture that keeps curls hydrated

If your curls get easily weighed down by product, this is your line! They invested in weightless formulas to keep your hair moisturized, and frizz-free but not weighed down. That's why in this line they've included a clarifying shampoo, and primer + gel as styling step. The Essential Moisture line includes ingredients such as:

🌱 Aloe Vera: Soothes the scalp keeping it hydrated and preventing irritation.
🌱 Rice Protein: Encourages volume and texture so curls don't fall flat.
🌱 Panthenol: Protects hair's cuticle to add volume, shine, and texture.
🌱 Wheat Protein: Helps to strengthen strands from within, helping to make them healthier, shinier, and more manageable.
🌱 Sericin: Helps to improve texture, strength, and shine.
🌱 Coconut Oil: Moisturizes and protects against frizz and split ends.
🌱 Argan Oil: Helps improve hair's elasticity, making it less prone to breakage.

ESSENTIAL MOISTURE CLAIMS

The Essential Moisture line claims weightless moisture that keeps curls hydrated, combining multi-tasking weightless formulas. This line includes:

Curl Gel "WEATHER UP GEL - THE NO-CRUNCH STYLER"

Curls are more affected by humidity due to their unique structure, making them susceptible to frizz. Finally, a high-performance gel that's formulated specifically to fight off every curl's worst nightmare! It creates defined curls and blocks out humidity-inducing frizz and flyaways—all without leaving curls feeling sticky or crunchy.

Clarifying shampoo "HIT RESET - BUILD-UP MINIMALIST"

When your curls get weighed down by product build-up, grime, and excess oils, it's time for a reset. This gentle clarifying shampoo is all about balance. Eliminate build-up without stripping curls of the natural oils they need to form and stay strong. In just one wash, it will help reboot the curl pattern for a fresh start.

Conditioner "WEATHER OR NOT - THE ELEMENT DEFIER"

Humidity is no threat to moisturized curls. This game-changing formula encourages curl definition and creates the perfect frizz-free foundation. By infusing nourishing moisture within strands and sealing the cuticle, frizz and humidity don't stand a chance.

Curl Primer "RALLY THE CURLS - THE DYNAMITE DEFINER"

This multitasking formula sets your curls up for success, working hard so you don't have to. The weightless primer boosts strength, enhances definition, increases elasticity, and reduces frizz. Help define and shape day-one curls or use them as an overnight treatment to restore your natural texture. Whatever your curls need, this powerful primer has got them covered.

Refreshing Spray "HYPE IT UP - THE SAVVY CURL FORMER"

When curls begin to droop on day-two or three (or four, or five...no judgment) this supercharged refreshing spray perks them right up. Chock full of botanicals and good-for-your-curls ingredients, the no-residue formula restores moisture, definition, and uniformity.

ESSENTIAL MOISTURE REVIEW

From this line my favorites are HIT RESET - Clarifying Shampoo and the WEATHER UP - Defining Gel.

The clarifying shampoo Hit Reset is gentle and weightless so curls that get weighed down by heavier formulas can stay healthy and hydrated. I simply love this clarifying shampoo, it is the best clarifying shampoo I've ever tested. My hair feels deeply clean after using it, but at the same time, it doesn't give that awful stripping sensation of over-cleaned hair that I hate. I found it amazing how it can deeply clean and be as soft at the same time, even my straight-haired boyfriend is obsessed with it (even tho I insist he stops wasting it with his "low-demand" hair 😅). The ease of application and smell is also very good. No downsides. I must say I'm VERY impressed with this shampoo, it's worth every penny!

The Weather up gel is very easy to apply, it has a geleé consistency that is not sticky at all, as some gels I've already tried before. Although, I'm not a "gel type of girl" I liked the result it gave to my hair, leaving it bouncy, and keeping the definition for a longer time!

Available at twisthair.com (9.99$/each) with FREE STANDARD SHIPPING on every order.

EXTRA MOISTURE - Lightweight moisture that replenishes parched curls

The Extra Moisture is an ultra-nourishing line that gives your curls the TLC they deserve to restore shine and improve your natural curl pattern. The formula includes natural ingredients such as:

ALL OF THE GOOD:

🌱 Rice Protein: enhances natural volume and helps to soften brittle or damaged hair.
🌱 Panthenol: seals moisture in and protects against environmental aggressors.
🌱 Capuacu Butter: adds luster and shine to curls.
🌱 Coconut Oil: moisturizes and softens strands.
🌱 Jojoba Oil: nourishes strands and helps improve natural curl pattern.
🌱 Shea Butter: helps lock in moisture without leaving hair heavy or greasy.
🌱 Grapeseed Oil: locks in moisture to prevent frizz, split ends, and brittle hair.
🌱 Vegetable protein: penetrates the hair shaft to help strengthen your weakest parts and protect them from breakage.
🌱 Chamomile Extract: nourishes strands leaving them shiny and lustrous.
🌱 Aloe Vera: conditions strands leaving them smooth and shiny.
🌱 Sweet Almond Oil: improves the texture of hair for maximum touch ability.

NONE OF THE BAD:

Free of Mineral Oil, Petrolatum, Parabens, Phthalates, Harsh Sulfates, and D4, D5, D6 Silicones.

EXTRA MOISTURE CLAIMS

The Extra Moisture line claims lightweight moisture that replenishes parched curls. This line is a boost of moisture to parched curls to keep them soft and manageable. It's perfect for curls that are thirsty for movement & definition, giving them bounce, hold, and lightweight moisture. This line includes the following products:

4-in-1 conditioner "GIMME IT ALL - THE 4-IN-1 BOSS" When your curls need hydration, this multi-use conditioner gets the job done. Quickly cleanse and condition in one rinse. For days when your style calls for a little more moisture, use this as your hydrating base to prep for styling. If curls are dehydrated and undefined, grab this and give your curls a twist.

Curl defining geleé "READY, SET, BOUNCE - THE BOLD BOOSTER" For those days when your hair needs some extra oomph, this curl-defining gelée encourages your natural bounce and definition. It also adds a boost of moisture to stave off static, leaving curls frizz- and crunch-free.

Hair mask "SUNDAY FEELS - THE CURL RESTORATION EXPERT" This ultra-nourishing mask gives your curls the TLC they deserve to restore shine and improve your natural curl pattern. Transform dry, damaged, stretched-out curls in as few as three minutes.

Oil infused shampoo "ON-REPEAT - THE DAILY DO-GOODER" For curls that require washing more frequently, use this shampoo that will cleanse without stripping strands of vital oils. This gentle oil-based formula infuses moisture into each strand and mimics curls' natural oils as you cleanse to help restore softness and shape while preventing frizz and flyaways.

Leave-in conditioner "CURL GOALS - THE HYDRATION FOUNDATION" This leave-in conditioner hydrates, detangles, and primes with the moisture that all curls crave—and without the weight that drags them down. Curls become more defined when you start with a strong, hydrated foundation before styling. So spray this on damp hair, then follow with the rest of your routine to reach today's curl goal.

Curl refresher and conditioning spray "FEELIN' THIRSTY - THE MOISTURE MIST-RESS" When droopy, dehydrated curls need a pick-me-up, this creamy rehydrating mist delivers an immediate moisture boost for a curl transformation as you've never seen. Smooth frizz, reform curls, and bring back definition between wash days—all with a few spritzes.

EXTRA MOISTURE REVIEW

I've been looking for a good shampoo for a long time, and every time I got disappointed because it was either too moisturizing for my curls or too cleaning. I've had a hard time trying to find one that had the perfect balance for my 3a, dry curls UNTIL I found the oil-infused shampoo ON-REPEAT. This shampoo has the PERFECT balance between moisture and cleaning for my hair, I almost feel I could even detangle my hair after using it because it feels so soft and hydrated, it also gives a freshness feeling to it that I love. I've also tested it on a one-week trip to the beach and it was a very good choice as it helped with moisture and it didn't dry even more my hair! Overall, I think Twist has the perfect balance for shampoos!

After falling in love with the ON-REPEAT shampoo, I tested the 4-in-1 GIMME IT ALL as a conditioner, and starting with its dense texture that gives rich hydration to the curls, it makes it easy to detangle and it makes the wash a more pleasing experience!

Available at twisthair.com (9.99$/each) with FREE STANDARD SHIPPING on every order.

ULTIMATE MOISTURE - Deep hydration to nourish and restore curls

With the Ultimate Moisture you'll experience improved manageability as it restores moisture to dry, damaged curls, it is deepy hydrating and hyper nourishing, made for the most parched of curls, thirsty for definition, elasticity, and nourishment. The ingredients:

ALL OF THE GOOD

🌱 Shea Butter: moisturizes strands and prevents frizz.
🌱 Jojoba Oil: nourishes curls to make them appear healthy and supple.
🌱 Pataua Fruit Oil: provides intense hydration to parched parts.
🌱 Aloe Vera: conditions curls leaving them soft and shiny.
🌱 Sweet Almond Oil: helps make curls touchably soft.
🌱 Castor Oil: helps to improve scalp health, promoting healthy and strong hair growth.
🌱 Coconut Oil: infuses moisture to add shine and softness while helping to reduce the appearance of split ends.
🌱 Grapeseed Oil: locks in moisture to prevent frizz and combat split ends and brittle strands.
🌱 Rice protein: encourages natural curl pattern.
🌱 Avocado Oil: penetrates the hair shaft to moisturize, strengthen, and protect strands.
🌱 Abyssinian Oil: adds brilliant shine to lackluster curls.

NONE OF THE UGLY

Free of Mineral Oil, Petrolatum, Parabens, Phthalates, Harsh Sulfates, and D4, D5, D6 Silicones. Safe for color and chemically treated hair

ULTIMATE MOISTURE CLAIMS

The Ultimate Moisture line claims deep hydration to nourish and restore curls, it is the master of delivering moisture to the thirstiest of curls to help build shape and definition. This intense hydration penetrates deep into strands, delivering a boost of moisture that lasts for days.
This line includes the following products:

Styling cream "BOSS BOUNCE - THE WHIPPED WORLD WONDER" Move over, there's a new boss in town. This lightweight, buildable cream melts into strands to deliver definition and hydration for touchable, frizz-free curls. It also works overtime to disguise flyaways and split ends.

2-in-1 conditioner and primer "PRIMED & CO. - THE HEAVY-DUTY DOER" Curls get primed, prepped, and ready for more (moisture). This ultra-rich nourishing primer doubles as a deeply hydrating daily conditioner. As a primer, it works to improve styling product performance by creating a moisturized base for definition. When used as your go-to conditioner, you'll experience improved manageability as it restores moisture to dry, damaged curls.

Multi-use oil "CURL REIGN - THE LIQUID GOLD SAVIOR" Unlock the secret to worship-worthy curls. Six miracle oils combine to deliver intense hydration, nourishment, protection and shine. This multi-tasking oil can be used on wet or dry hair as a conditioner, treatment, leave-in, or styler depending on what your curls need.

Co-wash "BIG, RICH, & DREAMY - THE LUX CO-WASH" This ultra-hydrating co-wash cleanses and conditions all at once! Gently remove buildup and impurities from your curls without stripping natural oils. This rich formula will leave you wondering, how can something so nourishing be so cleansing.

Hydrating Dry Oil "GLOW THE EXTRA MILE - THE CURL EXTENDER" When curls start to lose their pizzaz, bring them back to life with this style-reviving dry-oil spray that restores natural oils, adds shine, and helps them finish strong (until wash day, that is).

ULTIMATE MOISTURE REVIEW

From this line, I'm obsessed with the Primed & co, this product is a REAL heavy-duty doer, it has a dense and rich texture, and gives me all the moisture my hair needs, giving it a shiny and lustrous look! I've tried it as a primer and as a styling cream, I prefer and use it mostly as a styling cream, I don't feel the need to layer it with gel or mousse to complement its power! Tho if you have thin hair, be careful because as it's VERY moisturizing it can weigh down your hair! Better to start with a bit and go applying more as you feel the need, a little goes a long way when talking about this product.

Available at twisthair.com (9.99$/each) with FREE STANDARD SHIPPING on every order.
