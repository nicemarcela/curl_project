Story Time: How the Hair Industry Saved and Raised Curl Specialist Raquel Noriko.

#submited@raquelnorikocurlstudio

Raquel Noriko is a curly hair specialist based in Maui, HI. Her passion for people as well as curly hair is seen through her intuitive approach and cutting style to each client and their needs.

"I help women embrace their natural hair effortlessly one curly head at a time. I believe that our confidence as women starts with embracing ourselves at our core. For us naturally curly hair women, that begins with our hair." - Raquel Noriko
